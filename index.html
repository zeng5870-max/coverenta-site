02

Brand Story

Born from the idea that luxury should feel inevitable.

Coverenta began as a study in subtraction. Remove the trend, the excess, the ornamental gesture, and what remains must be stronger: fabric, proportion, movement, memory.

The house works in a restrained monochrome language, allowing cut and material to carry the emotion. Nothing is casual. Nothing is over-explained.
